About the 42nd

The 42nd Airborne Division is a dedicated Arma 3 milsim community focused on realistic military simulation, tactical teamwork, and brotherhood.

We operate as an Airborne Division — specializing in rapid deployment, vertical assault operations, and combined arms warfare. Our members train together, fight together, and build lasting bonds through shared operations.

Whether you're a seasoned milsim veteran or new to the community, the 42nd welcomes disciplined individuals who are ready to be part of something bigger than themselves.

OPERATIONS

What We Do

01

Main Operations

Large-scale, Zeus-led missions featuring combined arms warfare, multi-objective scenarios, and coordinated platoon-level assaults. Held weekly.

02

Training Exercises

Scheduled training sessions covering everything from basic infantry tactics to advanced specializations like CQB, convoy operations, and air assault.

03

Side Operations

Smaller, member-led missions run throughout the week. Great for sharpening skills and trying new roles in a lower-pressure environment.

04

Joint Operations

Combined operations with allied milsim units, bringing together larger forces for massive, multi-unit campaigns and special events.
